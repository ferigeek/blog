# Environment varialbes

- `SECRET_KEY`
- `ALLOWED_HOSTS` (seperated by comma)
- **Database**:
    - `DB_ENGINE`
    - `DB_NAME`
    - `DB_USER`
    - `DB_PASS`
    - `DB_PORT`
    - `DB_HOST`
    - *If not provided, **sqlite** will be used.*
- `TIME_ZONE` (Default: `UTC`)
